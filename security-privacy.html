Security and Privacy

What Data Is Collected

The app may process Jira account identifiers, display names, Jira group information, latest questionnaire answers, computed assessment results, dimension scores, pressure and collaboration indicators, timestamps, app settings, and aggregate team analytics.

Where Data Is Stored

App data is stored in Atlassian Forge Storage associated with the installed Forge app and Atlassian site. The standard Cloud version does not require an external database.

How Forge Storage Is Used

Forge Storage is used for app configuration, latest answer sets, computed snapshots, user profile metadata needed for app access, and aggregate-ready indexes used by team and executive views.

Who Can Access Results

Standard users can access their own results. Authorized PM, PO, HR, Executive, and Jira administrator roles can access role-specific and aggregate views according to Jira groups and app settings.

How Privacy Thresholds Work

When a selected scope has fewer completed responses than the configured threshold, aggregate charts are hidden to reduce the risk of identifying individuals.

How Data Is Protected

The app uses Forge platform controls, Forge Storage, Jira and Forge permissions, role-aware views, and privacy thresholds. Site administrators are responsible for assigning Jira groups and access roles appropriately.

Deletion And Support Requests

Users can request support, deletion, or privacy assistance at https://github.com/misaqyrn2019/TeamHealthApps/issues.

Not For Clinical Diagnosis Or Hiring Decisions

The app provides workplace wellbeing, work-style, collaboration, and team-level indicators. It must not be used for clinical diagnosis, medical assessment, hiring decisions, promotion decisions, disciplinary decisions, individual performance scoring, or automated employment decisions.
