Privacy Policy

Team Health and Personality Assessment for Jira stores app configuration, latest questionnaire responses, computed result snapshots, and role-aware analytics in Atlassian Forge Storage.

What Data Is Collected

The app may process Jira account identifiers, display names, Jira group information, latest questionnaire answers, computed assessment results, assessment timestamps, app settings, and aggregate team analytics.

Where Data Is Stored

App data is stored in Atlassian Forge Storage associated with the installed Forge app and Atlassian site. The standard Cloud version does not require an external database.

Access Control

Standard users can access their own results. Authorized PM, PO, HR, Executive, and Jira administrator roles can access role-specific and aggregate views according to Jira groups and app settings.

Privacy Thresholds

When a selected project, team, role, or filter scope has fewer completed responses than the configured threshold, aggregate charts are hidden to reduce the risk of identifying individuals.

Responsible Use

The app provides workplace wellbeing, work-style, collaboration, and team-level indicators. It is not intended for clinical diagnosis, medical assessment, hiring decisions, or automated employment decisions.

Deletion And Support Requests

Users can request support, deletion, or privacy assistance at https://github.com/misaqyrn2019/TeamHealthApps/issues.

Full details are available at Security and Privacy.
