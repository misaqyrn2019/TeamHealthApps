# Security And Privacy

Team Health and Personality Assessment for Jira stores app configuration, latest questionnaire responses, computed result snapshots, and role-aware analytics in Atlassian Forge Storage.

The app uses Jira and Forge permissions, privacy thresholds, and role-aware views to limit access to personal and team-level results.

Full security and privacy details are available here:

https://github.com/misaqyrn2019/TeamHealthApps/blob/main/SECURITY_PRIVACY.md

The app is not intended for clinical diagnosis, medical assessment, hiring decisions, or automated employment decisions.
