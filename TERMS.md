# Terms Of Service

## Purpose

Team Health and Personality Assessment for Jira provides workplace collaboration, work-style, and team-level analytics for Jira teams. The app is intended to support team communication, coaching, planning, and responsible organizational insight.

## Acceptable Use

Users and administrators agree to use the app only for workplace support and collaboration improvement.

The app must not be used for:

- Clinical diagnosis
- Medical or psychological assessment
- Hiring or rejection decisions
- Promotion, compensation, or disciplinary decisions
- Automated employment decisions
- Individual performance scoring

## Administrator Responsibilities

Jira administrators are responsible for:

- Installing and configuring the app
- Assigning Jira groups and access roles appropriately
- Setting privacy thresholds suitable for their organization
- Ensuring that named results are visible only to authorized users
- Responding to user requests for support or deletion
- Reviewing the app with internal privacy and compliance requirements

## User Responsibilities

Users should answer questionnaires honestly and treat results as workplace reflection signals. Results should be interpreted with context and should not be treated as fixed labels.
