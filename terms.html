Terms of Service

Team Health and Personality Assessment for Jira provides workplace collaboration, work-style, and team-level analytics for Jira teams. The app is intended to support team communication, coaching, planning, and responsible organizational insight.

Acceptable Use

The app must not be used for clinical diagnosis, medical assessment, hiring or rejection decisions, promotion decisions, disciplinary decisions, individual performance scoring, or automated employment decisions.

Administrator Responsibilities

Jira administrators are responsible for installing and configuring the app, assigning access roles appropriately, setting privacy thresholds, and ensuring that named results are visible only to authorized users.

User Responsibilities

Users should answer questionnaires honestly and treat results as workplace reflection signals. Results should be interpreted with context and should not be treated as fixed labels.
