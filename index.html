Team Health and Personality Assessment for Jira

Team Health and Personality Assessment for Jira helps Jira teams review workplace collaboration, work-style preferences, and team-level health indicators using privacy-aware assessment and analytics pages.

The app is intended for workplace insight, coaching, communication planning, and responsible team support. It is not intended for clinical diagnosis, hiring decisions, performance scoring, or automated employment decisions.
